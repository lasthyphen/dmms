Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question that is not answered? You can contact us at support@dijets.io.

What are DMMs?

DMMs is a collective word given to a suite of Finanial Platform Services i.e Dijetex Exchange, Rogue DeFi Platform and Enterprise Vaults that enable you to manage your digital assets and exchange crypto to fiat, manage on & off-chain expenses, and integrate with existing financial stacks in a secure, compliant and insured way. Some of the benefits of using DMMs are:

  • DMMs Dashboard for Unified crypto & fiat management
  • Instant on/off-ramps with automatic gains & losses calculations
  • ACH, wire, and check payments (Merchant Accounts)
  • Automated payments
  • Digital Asset Management across the DMM suite
  • …and much more

How do I get started with using the DMMs?

Fill out the sign up form to request early access and after being placed in a queue you will receive an email with a link to fill out the onboarding forms. A member of the team will be available for any help during the process.

What is the DMMs Dashboard?

The DMMs dashboard allows you to manage your digital assets across all of the Dijets Financial Suite services.

What do I need to apply for a personal & merchant account?

You will need to provide the following information to get started:

Personal Account: 1) Driving License or passport number, 2) Proof of identity (an image of your driver's license or passport)

Merchant Account: 1) Company Incorporation Certificate, 2) VAT Certificate, 3) Sole Proprietorship document (if applicable), 4) List of all the PSA (person with significant control), beneficial owners and their personal information

How fast is onboarding?

It takes less than 20 minutes to go through our onboarding process. After we receive your early request form, you will be placed on a queue to access the DMMs. For KYC purposes we may ask for further documentation and get back to you in less than 24 hours.

Can I access DMMs with non-custodial wallets?

DMMs are fully compatible with non-custodial wallets such as Dijets Decypher, Metamask and Gnosis Safe. Dijets aims to partner with custodial wallet services after the public launch to utilise the payment rails and on/off-ramps.

Will my funds be FSCS insured?

Fiat deposits will only be available after the public launch. Dijets is in talks with FSCS-insured banks and insurance brokers to store your digital assets safely.
