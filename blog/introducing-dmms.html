Dijetex Exchange

Imagine the possibilities of turning any real world asset into a 'Digital Asset'. Stocks, Options, Forex, Derivatives & so on.

The Ecosystem's Exchange, Dijetex lets you trade both - the digital and the 'Dijetal' assets. Its interface has purposefully been designed in a way that lets the information on the pages take centre stage rather than any unnecessary distractions for the users while they execute their trade or method strategies. Similarly to maintain our development rule of minimum-clicks-per-process we have gone to great lengths in order to achieve a powerful exchange capable of handling transactional and query loads.

Rogue DeFi Platform

Rogue Platform enables traders, investors, novices and experts to be able to mint, buy, sell and monitor digital assets like never before. The trading grounds section within Rogue lets you deploy DeFi recipes using plain english. Rogue can process Natural Language, deploy 'Methods', and manage your digital assets.

Users can manage multiple snapshots of their profiles across the Dijets Ecosystem and seamlessly connect directly to other DMM services for asset transfers and balances.

Enterprise-Grade Vaults

Dijets Enterprise-Grade Vaults service was built to improve the overall standard for digital asset management and ownership with smart contract accounts and integration. Vaults can be created with custom ownership policies and tied to Rogue's DeFi recipes for yields and earnings.
